# f101v — pharmaceutical (238 words)

TRANSLATION:

**Aromatic Oil Preparations**

Various aromatic oils and sweet preparations. For oil extraction: Take the oils and essences, combining them with filtering materials. Pour the mixture through cotton filters, applying gentle pressure. Rest the preparation until clear.

For medicinal ointments: Mix the base oils with aromatic substances. Heat gently while stirring. Pour through fine filters until the liquid becomes bright and clear like lamp oil. Test for proper consistency - it should flow smoothly when poured.

Advanced preparation: Take the filtered oil and add chalk powder for clarification. Mix with strong alcohol as solvent. The mixture should turn from bitter to sweet taste when properly completed. Apply gentle heating throughout the process.

Immersion treatment: For bathing applications, warm the aromatic oil preparation. Immerse in cycles, allowing proper soaking time. The oil should maintain its bright, clear quality throughout the bathing process.

Quality indicators: The finished preparation shows signs of completion when it pours smoothly, maintains clarity like lamp light, and achieves the proper sweet taste. Remove any sediment by careful filtering.

Storage and application: Keep the clear preparation in proper containers. For use, warm gently and apply by dropping or anointing. The oil should maintain its aromatic properties and bright appearance. Test each batch for proper consistency before application.

Final notes: Monitor the preparation for proper color, taste, and flow characteristics. Well-prepared aromatic oils will show clear signs of completion and maintain their medicinal properties when properly stored.
