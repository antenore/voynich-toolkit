TRANSLATION:

For the aromatic preparation, take the leaves in clear liquid. Take the pouring vessel and boil with heating. Remove and turn aside the liquid. Rest it in clear mixture, stirring with cotton in clear solution.

Apply cotton to the leaves, remove and filter. Heat with clear preparation repeatedly until clear and pure.

Prepare the mixture as a drink in clear liquid.

Prepare by anointing twice with filtering. Take in clear leaves as a strong preparation in clear solution.

Strong liquid with light and heating in clear soaking, clarifying and boiling the leaves with light in clear solution.

Filter and prepare the leaves as a strong solution.
