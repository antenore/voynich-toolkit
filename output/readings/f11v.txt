# f11v — herbal (50 words)

TRANSLATION:

To extract the essence: Rest the plant material and apply heat to the bright, clear preparation. Use soft filtering material with bright chalk to clarify the strong alcoholic solvent.

Sweeten the mixture - this preparation should yield the correct sweet taste when completed. Filter again through soft material, then pour the alcoholic solution into the preparation vessel.

For bathing application: Pour and set the mixture, then anoint repeatedly. Apply the medicinal solution and rest it properly.

Complete the drops by removing impurities, then pour the final preparation into storage.
