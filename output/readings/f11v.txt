# f11v — herbal (50 words)

TRANSLATION:
To extract the essence from the plant: Take the clear bright portion and filter through soft cotton material. Pour the bright mixture through chalk clarifier into strong alcohol solvent. Anoint and apply the preparation - when the mixture becomes bitter, continue stirring with cotton filtration. 

The completed preparation should be poured through filtering material into alcohol. When heated, pour drop by drop into warm solution. Continue the soaking process, then pour and filter repeatedly. Rest and apply medicine as needed. Remove any residue by turning aside and pouring through clean filtering material.
