# f13v — herbal (71 words)

TRANSLATION:

The plant's stems should be cut and prepared into mixtures, then softened and prepared with applications. Death occurs from bitter preparations when softened without proper filtering through cotton material.

The mixture should be prepared and applied with soaking treatments.

From these, pour the liquid while it is warm, rest it, then apply it as a medicine while it is bright.

Circle around the bright, clear preparation - anoint from above, apply the medicine properly, and purify with clear preparations.

Clear, sweet mixtures should be prepared with strong alcohol solutions that are bright and well-refined.

Cut while bright and clear, remove the bitter residue, strain it properly within the bright vessel.

Cut the soft material, sweeten it with applications - this medicine should be sweet, soft, and properly anointed for application.

From this mixture, pour the prepared solution when bitter.
