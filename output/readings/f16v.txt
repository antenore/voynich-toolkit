# f16v — herbal (77 words)

Filter with the bright preparation. Cleanse thoroughly with the filtered mixture, adding bitter elements. The strong preparation should be anointed with soft filtering material in clear liquid.

Rest and apply the filtered medicine. Apply the soft material in very bright conditions. Rest in the temple vessel. Filter with bright preparation. In the filtering vessel, add medicine, anoint with bitter preparation.

Rest in the house twice. In the house, anoint with soft preparation, filtering with bright preparation and cleansing.

Filter the mixture with bright preparation, filtering in warm conditions with cleansing application.

In the filtering process, filter between applications.

In bright conditions, set the mixture and cleanse.

The filtering process completes.
