# f17r — herbal (80 words)

TRANSLATION:

To extract the clear resin from this plant: First, cut and gather the roots and stems. Remove the outer bark and fibrous material using cotton filtering. Rest the material and apply gentle heat.

Pour the preparation into a mixture with chalk to clarify. Add strong drink as solvent. When the liquid becomes bright and clear, pour and set aside. The bitter taste indicates proper extraction.

Take cotton and apply the mixture with chalk. Soak thoroughly until soft. For application, heat gently in warm conditions with roses. Allow to drip slowly.

Remove carefully and strain through cotton. The preparation should rest until it becomes bright and limpid.

To complete: Take the filtered liquid and pour slowly. Heat gently with sweet additions. When the mixture becomes clear and bright, it is ready.

Apply by anointing or soaking. The cotton should be saturated with the bright preparation. Store in a warm place with rose additions.

The final preparation will be clear, sweet to taste, and ready for medicinal use. Filter through cotton one final time before application.
