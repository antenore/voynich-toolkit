# f17r — herbal (80 words)

TRANSLATION:

**Herbal Extraction for Soft-Leaved Plant**

Remove the outer leaves and fibrous stems from the soft-leaved plant. Take the inner portions and crush them thoroughly. Filter the crushed material through cotton to extract the clear liquid essence.

Heat gently while stirring the mixture. Add chalk powder to clarify the preparation. When the liquid becomes bright and clear, pour into a vessel for settling. The bitter taste indicates proper extraction.

Apply the filtered preparation by anointing the affected area. Allow to rest and absorb. For stronger effect, mix with alcohol as solvent. The preparation should be applied warm using gentle drops.

For bathing treatment, immerse in warm water mixed with the plant essence. Soak repeatedly in cycles until the skin shows clearing signs. Remove when the bitter quality diminishes and sweetness appears - this indicates completion.

The finished preparation can be stored and applied as drops for continued treatment. When properly made, it will show bright, limpid qualities and provide the healing wonder-signs expected from this plant remedy.
