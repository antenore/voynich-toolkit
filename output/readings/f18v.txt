# f18v — herbal (72 words)

TRANSLATION:

Take aromatic herbs and fragrant roots with strong drink as the head mixture. Drop this into good water, let it rest and settle into preparations repeatedly. Let it rest and settle the preparations, then strain the liquid. Prepare the mixture and strain it with bitter herbs.

For the dropping of preparations from the mixture, use cotton as a bright filter. Take and anoint the preparation, then drip the mixture with the liquid preparations. Use cotton to remove the sediment from the bright liquid, removing the bitter residue.

From the mixture, clarify it brightly, making the sweet preparations clear. Strain the preparation and mixture through the strong liquid. The liquid should rest as a clear, bright filtered solution.
