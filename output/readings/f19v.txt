# f19v — herbal (81 words)

TRANSLATION:

For all soft filtering: take fallen matter, pour it into three vessels. In the vessels place cotton, mix with fallen matter. The vessels should contain clear, bright cotton within the mixture. Pour clear, bright liquid through each filter. From the vessels, pour clear liquid continuously. Place cotton in the vessels, take the filtered liquid.

Pour the strained cotton mixture through fallen matter again. Place it back, mix with sweet cotton, then strain again. Pour clear liquid from the bright vessels into containers. Clear liquid should be strained through cotton until bright and filtered, with clear sediment at bottom.

Drop the cotton vessels' liquid into containers. Apply mixture to bright cotton until settled, then strain the bright liquids. Cotton should be placed for clear straining.
