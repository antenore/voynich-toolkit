TRANSLATION:

Regarding the preparation of medicines and their proper application to the body through various treatments:

When preparing medicinal compounds, first filter through cotton material and allow to rest. Apply the clear preparations to affected areas, ensuring proper brightness and clarity of the liquid mixture. Pour the preparation and set it with chalk clarifier as needed.

For strong medicinal drinks, test for the proper bitter taste initially, then sweeten until the completion taste is achieved when the process is finished. Use proper lighting during preparation.

The bathing treatments require warm immersion cycles. Remove impurities and apply rose preparations by carefully dripping the medicine. Turn aside any unclear portions.

For internal medicines, pour the clear preparations and filter repeatedly until bright and limpid. The alcohol-based solvents should be mixed with the sweet preparations, ensuring proper consistency through multiple filtering stages.

Apply warm treatments to the body, allowing medicines to penetrate properly. The completed preparations should show the proper signs when ready - clear brightness and sweet taste indicating successful completion.

For external applications, anoint the affected areas with the prepared mixtures. Use cotton filtering material to strain all preparations. The warm soaking treatments should be applied in cycles, with proper removal of any bitter or unclear portions.

Complete the preparation by testing the final mixture for proper qualities - brightness, sweetness, and clarity. Store properly prepared medicines away from light until needed for application.
