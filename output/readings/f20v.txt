# f20v — herbal (91 words)

TRANSLATION:

Take this plant and prepare a decoction by resting it, then pour and mix it with soft filtering material. Apply the clear preparation as medicine.

When it becomes bright, filter through cotton. When clear, strain the liquid through fine cotton until it becomes limpid and bright.

Mix it well, and when the cotton filters it, strain again. Mix and rest the preparation thoroughly.

When it is bright and clear from filtering,

strain it well through cotton, squeezing it. When clear, filter with strong drink/alcohol, mix it well with the clear preparation.

In the morning, apply it as medicine. In the morning, pour it when clear, filtering through fine materials for brightness.

Apply it as medicine when it is clear. Pour bright liquid into the clear vessel.

When bright, mix it well and when it is this clear preparation in the vessel.

Mix it. Prepare it well and apply as clear medicine for the vessel.

When it becomes bright, apply as medicine through cotton for clear bright preparation, anoint with the mixture.

Thus pour and apply as medicine to anoint.
