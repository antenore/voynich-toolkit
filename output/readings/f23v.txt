# f23v — herbal (98 words)

TRANSLATION:

For the aromatic plant, gather the fragrant leaves when the moon is bright. Filter the soft material and heat gently, then apply to the affected area.

Take the plant and prepare a mixture by pouring carefully. Filter the bitter extract through cotton, then strain again. 

When clear, store the preparation. The sign of completion appears when the liquid drips bright and clear through the cotton filter.

Pour slowly, removing any residue. When bitter, add sweetener and strain again until clear.

Remove by pouring, then apply the medicine when bitter. Let it rest properly.

The treatment should be applied by soaking when the preparation is clear. Add warming herbs and let rest until bright.

Store in a vessel when clear, adding the medicine when bright. Heat for five portions.

In the container, strain the mixture until clear. Add chalk if needed, filter the bitter preparation with chalk.

Rest the medicine, strain and filter it. When sweet, pour and heat, then drip carefully.

Remove the mixture from the treatment vessel. Heat the preparation and let it rest completely.

Apply to the container when warm, storing it within. The drops should be clear within.

When properly prepared, this should produce clear drops for the wonderful treatment.
