# f24v — herbal (92 words)

TRANSLATION:

For extracting the essence from fresh plants: Take the leafy portions and aromatic parts, placing them in vessels with water. Let them rest and apply gentle heat while adding wine and good oil, along with other extractive substances.

From the pressed material, allow drops to fall and collect the liquid essence. When it becomes clear and bright, bitter to taste, separate the pure portion in its vessel, making it limpid and clean.

Cover it in its vessel and let it settle. 

The preparation should not be too thin or thick.

The refined extract yields a sweet, completed mixture.

Place it in containers with the essence well-separated among portions.

Apply this preparation - it works effectively when clear and bright.

When you see the signs appearing and the vessel shows the proper indicators, pour out portions of the medicine. The sweet, elevated essence should be removed.

Heat gently, applying the clear preparation repeatedly.

The strong preparation should be stored properly.

Heat it until it becomes bright.
