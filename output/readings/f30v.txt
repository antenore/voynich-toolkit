For bright/clear extraction preparation: In clear vessels, pour and prepare the filtered mixture. Rest in bright containers. 

Anoint the mixture. Pour from the anointed preparation. The strong drink mixture should be bright and clear. Filter through cotton until bright and clear. The bright liquid should be bitter initially.

Pour again until bright. Anoint and set aside. Rest until the preparation is bright.

In bright vessels, rest the filtered preparation. Pour the mixture and rest.

Rest the filtered preparation in vessels. Apply to vessels repeatedly.

Set the mixture in vessels repeatedly. Filter through preparation filters.

Rest the preparation in vessels and set in containers repeatedly.

Rest and apply to the filtered mixture. Pour again until bright.

Anoint and pour from the filtered mixture.

In vessels, apply the filtered preparation. Pour again until bright.

From the bright filtered preparation, rest and filter.
