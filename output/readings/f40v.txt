# f40v — herbal (107 words)

TRANSLATION:

For bright clear preparations, cut and arrange the plant material. Heat thoroughly in a vessel with water until it becomes concentrated. Filter the liquid through cotton while hot, then pour into containers. When cooling, rest and apply medicine as needed.

Take the clear preparation and pour repeatedly through bright filters. Soak and immerse, then pour into vessels. Heat the mixture and pour through fine cotton filters, adding water as needed. 

Continue pouring the bright liquid through cotton until clear, then set aside. When the preparation becomes sweet-tasting and bright, strain through filters into vessels. Heat and pour the bright clear liquid through fine cotton.

Add water and pour the mixture repeatedly, filtering until bright and clear. Drop by drop, pour and filter until the liquid becomes limpid. Set the preparation aside until sweet, then pour through cotton into final containers.

Pour the heated mixture and filter repeatedly until achieving the desired bright, clear quality. Continue dropping and filtering the preparation through cotton until the liquid shows proper brightness and sweet completion taste.

For final preparation, heat gently and pour through filters, allowing drops to fall slowly until the mixture achieves optimal clarity and sweetness for medicinal application.
