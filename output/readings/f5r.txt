# f5r — herbal (56 words)

TRANSLATION:

Bathe the leaves properly, then soften and crush them while fresh. Rest and apply the preparation through proper steeping.

Pour and set in a vessel with liquid. The heated mixture should be poured onto the softened plant material.

When properly done, make a bright, clear preparation by repeated pouring. This mixture should be strong and well-prepared.

The bright preparation can be bathed in or applied directly as needed.

Rest the bathed preparation with warming liquid and pour it while bright and clear.

Then pour the mixture repeatedly through multiple filtration steps. This completes the resting preparation process.

The final poured mixture should be set and the preparation is ready for bathing applications.
