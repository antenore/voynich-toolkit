# f65v — herbal (46 words)

TRANSLATION:

Take the plant and prepare it with strong drink, pour into a vessel of soft material. Make it bright and clear, strain from the liquid and heat gently for the preparation, completing the mixture.

Sweet taste indicates proper preparation. Rest and apply the medicine as needed.

For bathing treatment: soak and pour the preparation into warm water for immersion cycles. The soaking liquid should be applied in the heating bath.

When applying the medicine, rest after each treatment. Take the plant preparation and mix it with the clarified liquid for final application.
