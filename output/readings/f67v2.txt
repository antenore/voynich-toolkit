# f67v2 — cosmological (64 words)

TRANSLATION:

In the brightness of creation, establish the foundation with chalk. Take the soft material, pour and turn aside, go with the soft preparation of brightness. In the clear, clear pourings, pour the setting. Pour forth and set from the right hand. From the foundations, go with the elevated ones and the chalk. From the soft, strong clarifier, set in chalk. The elevated ones pour down from above, from the water, completing preservation. The wonder and the soft foundation, from death, chalk. In the living, from the completed, water in the elevations, foundation. Pour forth and the preparations. The waters and the breath, the clear brightness. Foundation and sweetness, the mixture. Set and beauty, and their completions. The dark luminous ones, go completing. Behold, the lamps of the heights. The living ones pour down from above. The circling ones in the elevated preparations. In the living ones, complete. The elevated ones, from the living ones.

This appears to be a cosmological text describing the celestial ordering and flow of elements from above to below, with emphasis on clarity, brightness, and the downward pouring of cosmic substances through various levels of creation.
