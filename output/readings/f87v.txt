# f87v — herbal (104 words)

TRANSLATION:
To prepare bright clear extract: take the fresh shoots and bathe them, then pound and grind with soft material. Pour the strained liquid into a vessel. Set aside the pressed material and pour again. Strain the second pressing and set with the first portion. Pour the combined mixture into a container with water, setting it in a warm place. Take it up with cumin root, then pour the liquid into a prepared vessel. 

Pour the extract with the bitter portion into a measuring cup. Take from the preparation as needed. Pour into a suitable container, setting with clear water until the mixture becomes sweet to taste. This completes the basic preparation.

Apply with soft material to affected areas between the ribs where there are signs of inflammation. The warmth within brings forth healing properties. When the brightness appears as a sign, the treatment shows its effect.
