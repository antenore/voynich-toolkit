# fRos — cosmological (338 words)

TRANSLATION:

In the beginning of creation, the bright emanations flow forth. The divine light rests and anoints the celestial spheres. Within the cosmic order, the luminous essence pours through multiple levels - from the highest realm down through the intermediary domains to the lower spheres.

The celestial waters bathe and immerse each sphere in cycles of purification. Through warm emanations, the divine light drips and flows, creating signs and wonders throughout the cosmic structure. The bright preparations mix with the clear substances, forming the fundamental elements.

Each sphere receives its portion of the divine influx through measured pourings and applications. The cosmic waters flow in patterns, creating the basic matrix from which all emanations emerge. The light passes through filtering stages, becoming refined and clarified at each level.

The upper realms contain the pure, sweet essence, while the lower domains receive increasingly dense manifestations. Through continuous cycles of bathing and soaking, each cosmic level maintains its proper luminosity and function.

The divine light operates through specific channels and vessels, distributing its influence according to cosmic law. The spheres turn and remove impurities through their rotational movements, maintaining the clarity of the celestial waters that flow between realms.

This cosmic process creates the fundamental structure through which divine influence reaches all levels of creation, from the highest spiritual domains down to the material world, each receiving its appropriate measure of light and sustenance.
